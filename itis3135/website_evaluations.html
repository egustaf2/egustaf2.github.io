Website Evaluation

Yale School of Art

This is the home page for the Yale School of Art. This page is interesting because it is an ongoing collaborative experiment where all member of the School of Art community have the ability to add new content to the webpage!

  • Contrast: The contrast of this website is all over the place. The different colors and boxes are all over the places which makes it look out of order.
  • Repetition: There is no repetition on any of the pages within the website. Every page seems to be random and nothing really fits together.
  • Alignment: The pages are left aligned and ell of the elements seem like they are randomly put on the page.
  • Proximity: There is little space between elements. It is hard to make out where to locate different pages with in the website.

Apple

This is the apple website

  • Contrast: The website has a good mount of contrast. The background color, the color of the text, the sizing, and the sections blend nicely together.
  • Repetition: The font syles, colors, and sections are repeated throught the whole website. The links at the top of the page are consistent with every page within the website.
  • Alignment: Everything on the pages are aligned nicely. Wverything is aligned in the center of the webpage which makes it easy to follow and look at.
  • Proximity: There is a great amount of spacing between each element on the webpage. It is not too crowded and it is not overwhelming to look at.
